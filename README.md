# PleasureCraft - Build Your Bedroom, Play the Game!

Escape the ordinary with PleasureCraft! A stunning collection of new female villagers and outfits ready to add a little magic to your Minecraft world.
This mod is all about building your dream bedroom and experiencing the game simultaneously! It includes a huge collection of themed bedroom scenes, each with unique lighting and decorations to enhance your building experience.
